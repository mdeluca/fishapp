Rapala - X-Rap

Features

  • Slashbait Action
  • Suspending on Pause
  • Textured Translucent Body

The X-Rap® is "all about the action." The versatility of the X-Rap allows an angler to impart a number of fish-catching actions in a single retrieve. Casts like a bullet for long accurate casts. Prominent scales and lateral line on the fuselage capture and flash light like a beacon. It can be fished both in an aggressive "Slashbait®" technique or with a classic Rapala® wobble.
